Mike Pondsmith

Cyberpunk was a warning, not an aspiration. Mike Pondsmith

This decade has many dystopian titles, books, movies, and games. So pick your poison. The legends of Cyberpunk have reared their heads to from the misty fog to reveal their revelations to us all: Mike Pondsmith, the creator of the pen and paper role-playing game Cyberpunk 2020 . He is working on a new project collaborating with CD Project Red to unveil the Cyberpunk 2077 video game. However, its introduction to the world has been rough and rocky, filled with many memes. Nevertheless, cyberpunk is inching its way to becoming a great game.

Where It All Began

Born to a psychologist and military air force officer, he often found himself globetrotting under the American banner in his youth. However, Mike Pondsmith's imagination enabled him to craft games in his spare time as a child. Eventually, it led him to his passion. Hence, his introduction to pen and paper role-playing games in college at the University of California, Davis, graduating with a BA in graphic design and a BS in behavioral psychology. His friend gave him a copy of Dungeon and Dragons (D&D), but the science-fiction role-playing game Traveller piqued his interest published in 1977.
